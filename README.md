# Proessences Inc. — Premium Fragrance Sourcing & Educational Platform

A sophisticated, full-stack React and Tailwind CSS application built for **PROESSENCES INC.**, the premier Supplier and Distributor of premium perfume oils, fragrance oils, essential oils, and aromatic raw materials in the Philippines.

---

## 📖 Module & Page Overview

The platform is designed with a premium, high-contrast, editorial aesthetic pairing refined serif typography (**Space Grotesk** or standard elegant serif headings) with responsive **motion** (Framer Motion) animations.

### 🏛️ 1. Home Dashboard (`HomeTab`)
The digital storefront of Proessences. Highlights core scent solution offerings, interactive product categorization (Fine Fragrances, Personal Care, Home Care), and prompt inquiry triggers.

### 🏢 2. About Us & Facility Tour (`AboutTab`)
A professional visual corporate profile detailing our exclusive local partnership with **Carvansons UK**:
*   **Virtual Office & Warehouse Tour**:
    *   Features a custom interactive video player supporting full-screen, responsive scrubbing, play/pause controls, and volume adjustments.
    *   Reads natively from localized high-resolution media: `public/office-video/office-video.mp4`.
    *   Displays a customized premium unbranded workspace cover image: `public/office-video/cover-page-office.jpg`.
*   **Corporate Values Matrix**: Focuses on *Customer Commitment*, *Quality Sourcing*, *Integrity & Compliance*, *Collaborative Teamwork*, *Respect for People*, and *Continual Development*.
*   **Affiliations & Compliance**: Highlights global audited manufacturing benchmarks (**IFRA**, **ISO 9001:2015**, **Halal certification**, **British Society of Perfumers**).

### 🧪 3. Fragrance Materials Knowledge Hub (`GuideTab`)
A comprehensive educational handbook detailing raw aromatic components designed to assist local cosmetics, wellness, and wellness product developers with smart sourcing selections:
*   **Natural Fragrances**: Traditional distillations, natural botanical characters, extraction processes, and seasonal variation profiles.
*   **Essential Oils**: Concentrated botanical extractions (e.g., Lavender, Eucalyptus, Lemongrass) and considerations for commercial formulation.
*   **Fragrance Oils**: Scientifically balanced, custom-compounded scent profiles offering superior batch predictability, scent variety (floral, woody, luxury), and formulation stability.
*   **Synthetic Aroma Materials**: Controlled laboratory ingredients providing high performance, shelf-life integrity, and structural supply reliability.
*   **Comparative Assessment (Natural vs. Synthetic)**: A balanced guide supporting hybrid product-development strategies.
*   **Material Selection Checklist**: Interactive parameter tracking covering target market alignment, allergen constraints, base compatibility, and supply scaling.

### 📬 4. Technical Advisory Desk (`ContactTab` & `SamplesTab`)
Enables local product formulators and commercial buyers to request wholesale sampling batches, inquire regarding custom scent synthesis, and register technical consultations.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack & Styling

*   **Framework**: React 18+ with Vite
*   **Animation**: `motion` (imported from `motion/react`)
*   **Styling**: utility-first Tailwind CSS
*   **Icons**: Lucide React
*   **Media Channels**: Localized unbranded high-fidelity photo-assets and MP4 video player overlays
